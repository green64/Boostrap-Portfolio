About Me

As a redheaded, left-handed Scorpio, I was destined to do things differently. I started my career in print publishing, but once I learned HTML, the immediate gratification of making a correction and seeing it online in seconds was much better than waiting for books to come back from the printer.

I wish I would've done something like boot camp 10 years ago. I really enjoy it and can't wait to put what I'm learning to use. I'm ready to move 100% to the IT side, but I think it'll still come in handy to understand SEO, cross-channel campaigns, etc. Even if you aren't actively using a language or skill, it's still helpful to understand the lexicon and the synergies between technologies.

When I'm not learning code I'm usually at a concert, because I love live music. The next show I have tickets for is a first-time festival called Homecoming in Cincinnati that's being put on by my favorite band, The National. The two-day lineup includes:

  • Father John Misty
  • Big Thief
  • Future Islands
  • Lord Huron
  • Plus more bands TBA

It's going to be an amazing show! And if that's not enough, another band I love, The Decemberists, has a new album and tour coming in March. I've already got tickets to see them in May at The Uptown, which should be great too.
